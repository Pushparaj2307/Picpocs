A Gallery of Birds

Eagle

Majestic raptor with powerful wings, sharp talons, and keen eyesight, symbolizing strength and freedom. Found in various habitats worldwide.

Owl

Nocturnal bird of prey with a silent flight, large forward-facing eyes, and sharp talons. Known for wisdom and hunting abilities, found in diverse habitats.

Peacock

Large, flamboyant bird with iridescent plumage and a distinctive fan-shaped tail, known for courtship displays. Native to South Asia but introduced worldwide.

Crow

Black bird known for intelligence and adaptability, found globally in various habitats. Recognizable cawing call. symbolizing both wisdom and death. Can thrive in urban environments, scavenging for food.

Sparrow

Small, brown bird common in urban and rural areas worldwide. Recognized for its cheerful chirping call. Adapted to human environments, nesting in buildings and foraging for seeds and insects.

Parrot

Colorful bird with curved beak and zygodactyl feet, found in tropical regions worldwide. Known for mimicry and intelligence, forming strong bonds with humans as pets.

Crane

Graceful bird with long legs and neck, found in wetlands and grasslands worldwide. Known for elaborate courtship displays and loud bugling calls. Symbolizes longevity and fidelity in many cultures.

Piegion

Common urban bird with gray plumage and iridescent neck feathers. Often seen in cities worldwide, known for cooing calls and ability to adapt to diverse environments.

Seagull

Coastal bird with white plumage, webbed feet, and distinctive squawking call. Often seen near beaches and harbors, scavenging for food scraps and riding ocean winds effortlessly.
