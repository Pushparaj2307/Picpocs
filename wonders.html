Seven Wonders of World


Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome, Italy, is a historic amphitheater renowned for its grandeur and significance. Built over 2,000 years ago, it hosted gladiatorial contests and other spectacles, attracting thousands of spectators. It stands as a symbol of ancient Roman engineering .

The Great Wall

The Great Wall of China, an architectural marvel spanning over 13,000 miles, is one of the world's most iconic landmarks. Built over centuries by various dynasties, it served as a formidable defense against invasions and symbolized China's strength and unity.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a majestic marble mausoleum located in Agra, India, is a symbol of eternal love and architectural brilliance. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is renowned for its beauty and intricate craftsmanship.

Christ Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer, a towering statue of Jesus Christ, overlooks the city of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, from the summit of Mount Corcovado. Standing at 98 feet tall, with arms outstretch it symbolizes peace, love, and faith. It become an iconic symbol of Brazil

Machu Pichu

Petra

Petra, nestled in the desert canyons of Jordan, is an ancient city renowned for its stunning rock-cut architecture and archaeological significance. Carved into rose-colored sandstone cliffs, the city flourished as the capital of the Nabatean Kingdom around 300 BCE to 100 CE.
